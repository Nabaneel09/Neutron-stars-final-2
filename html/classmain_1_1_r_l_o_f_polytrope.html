Detailed Description

Four-parameter piecewise-polytropic EOS of Read-Lackey-Owen-Friedman (2009), Section II B.

Encodes the three-piece polytropic core plus a simplified soft crust and provides the forward map , the inverse map , and the first-law-consistent total energy density needed by the TOV integrator.
