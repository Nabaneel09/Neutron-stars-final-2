\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Four-\/parameter piecewise-\/polytropic EOS of Read-\/\+Lackey-\/\+Owen-\/\+Friedman (2009), Section II B. 

Encodes the three-\/piece polytropic core plus a simplified soft crust and provides the forward map $P(\rho)$, the inverse map $\rho(P)$, and the first-\/law-\/consistent total energy density $\varepsilon(\rho)$ needed by the TOV integrator.

The three core pieces obey   \[             P = K_i\,\rho^{\Gamma_i}
\] on
\begin{DoxyItemize}
\item piece 1\+: $\rho_{cc} < \rho \le \rho_1$ with $\Gamma_1$,
\item piece 2\+: $\rho_1 < \rho \le \rho_2$ with $\Gamma_2$,
\item piece 3\+: $\rho > \rho_2$ with $\Gamma_3$. The $K_i$ are fixed by continuity of $P$ at each boundary together with $P(\rho_1)=P_1$.
\end{DoxyItemize}

\begin{DoxyNote}{Note}
The specific internal energy $e(\rho)$ is obtained from the first law $de = -P\,d(1/\rho)$ so that $\varepsilon = \rho c^{2} + \rho e$ enters the TOV equations self-\/consistently. This \texorpdfstring{$\sim$}{\string~}10 \% correction is what shifts $M_{\max}$ from its Newtonian value to the observed neutron-\/star scale. 
\end{DoxyNote}
